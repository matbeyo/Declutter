Privacy Policy

Effective as of 2026-03-06

This privacy policy applies to the Declutter app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Mattan (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not directly collect any personally identifiable information.

The Application accesses photo metadata on your device (such as file name, date, file size, and file path) solely for the purpose of displaying and helping you organize and delete your photos. This data is read from your device's media storage and is never transmitted off your device.

The Application stores the following preferences locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Onboarding completion state
  • Theme preference (system, light, or dark)
  • Cleaning statistics (photos cleaned, storage freed, streak count)

This preference data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server or third party.

The Application does not gather information about the location of your mobile device.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — The Application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and general usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's privacy policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — The Application uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as app opens, screen views, and general engagement metrics. This helps the Service Provider understand how the Application is used and improve it. Firebase Analytics may collect device information such as device model, operating system version, and a unique app-instance identifier. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — The Application uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data. When the Application crashes, Crashlytics automatically collects information about the crash, including device state, device model, operating system version, and stack traces. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. The Service Provider encourages you to review Google's privacy policy for more details on how your data is handled by these services.

Advertising

The Application displays advertisements through Google AdMob, including banner ads and native ads. These ads may be personalized based on your interests, or non-personalized based on contextual information. Ad networks may collect and use your advertising identifier and other device information to serve relevant ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings (Settings > Google > Ads on Android devices).

Permissions

The Application requests the following permissions:

  • Read Media Images (Android 13+) or Read External Storage (Android 12 and below) — required to access and display your photos so you can review and delete them
  • Vibrate — used for haptic feedback when swiping photos
  • Internet — required for displaying advertisements and sending analytics and crash reports to Firebase

The Application does not request camera, location, contacts, or microphone permissions.

Data Retention Policy

Local preferences are stored on your device and are removed when you uninstall the Application.

Data collected by third-party services (Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob) is retained according to their respective data retention policies. The Service Provider does not have direct control over data retained by these third-party services. For more information, see Google's data retention policies.

Children

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The Application displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, which are subject to Google's policies regarding children's content.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact the Service Provider at support@mattan.dev so that they can take the necessary actions. You must also be at least 16 years of age to consent to the processing of your personally identifiable information in your country (in some countries we may allow your parent or guardian to do so on your behalf).

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Your photo metadata and preferences are processed and stored locally on your device. Data transmitted to third-party services (Firebase, AdMob) is handled according to Google's security standards and encryption protocols.
